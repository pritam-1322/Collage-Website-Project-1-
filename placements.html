Placement Department

The Central Placement Cell was established in 2006 to cater the needs of students to face the competitive job market. Student counselling and career services have become the need of “Hour” for the students graduating in wide variety of disciplines. Shivaji University at present is recognized for educational excellence in the fields of Arts, Commerce, Science& Technology, Engineering, Interdisciplinary sciences and also in the Research & Development. The motto of Shivaji University is “Where Knowledge is Nectar” and is to provide learning blended with excellence, to create leaders of tomorrow. The dynamic and constantly evolving academic programmers reflect Shivaji University’s commitment to stay in tune with the expanding frontiers of knowledge worldwide. The extra-curricular activities are also treated with equal importance for the holistic development of students so that they can take on challenges faced in the competitive corporate world.
