Board Of Technical Education Rajasthan Result Declared on techedu.rajasthan.gov.in (1st 2nd 3rd Year)

BTER Diploma Result 2021 techedu.rajasthan.gov.in 1st 2nd 3rd Year Polytechnic Results Date & Time: – Board of Technical Education, Rajasthan is popularly known as BTER Jodhpur is going to declare Rajasthan Polytechnic Diploma Final Year Result 2021 in October 2021 (Expected Date).Exact date to release the BTER Jodhpur 1st 2nd 3rd Year Result (Engineering, Non-Engineering, Fashion Designing & Hotel Management and Catering Technology) will be available online here after official release at www.techedu.rajasthan.gov.in. You can offline news about BTER Result Date for Third Year Diploma published in newspapers like Dainik Bhaskar and Rajasthan Patrika etc.

How to check your Results

  • First of all visit official website of the Board of Technical Education Rajasthan Jodhpur i.e. www.techedu.rajasthan.gov.in.
  • Now on the homepage of the website click on the link “Exams/Results” in the menu bar.
  • After that, a new page will open in the same tab.
  • Click there “Diploma Results” link.
  • Now click on the link result of Sept October final year exams.
  • A new page will open in a new tab where enter Roll Number, Enrollment Number & Name.
  • Click on the “Submit” button.
  • Now your mark sheet subject wise will appear on this page.
